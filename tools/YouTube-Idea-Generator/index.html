Tips for Boosting YouTube CTR and Retention

Securing prime real estate within the video suggestion feed requires consistent execution across two core metrics: Click-Through Rate (CTR) and Audience Retention. Your thumbnail should present a compelling curiosity gap, while the headline validates that query without repeating the exact same text block.
